About

The Delaware Lacrosse Club is the University of Delaware's club level men's lacrosse team. We are an entirely student-run club which competes in the first division of the NCLL. This means that we play against programs at other mid-atlantic area colleges.

Our club places a focus on athletic, social, and philanthropic efforts. As a team, our mission is to win games and uphold principles of good sportsmanship and as a club, our aim is to create a co-curricular environment which provides a memorable and meaningful college experience.

Try Out

2019 Fall try outs are now confirmed. Tryouts are scheduled for Tuesday, 9/3 from 5:30-7:30 p.m. on Frazer 4, Wednesday, 9/4 from 9:30-11:00 p.m. at the Field House, and Thursday, 9/5 from 3:00-5:00 at Frazer Four. Check the bottom of the "Schedule" tab of this website for directions to the fields. You will likely need a ride to get to the field house, the easiest way to get there is by bus, either the North/South Academy Line, or the North/South College Ave. Line. You should plan on bringing your full gear to school with you to try out.

Tryouts will occur at the beginning of each semester. Tryouts take place over the course of three days, during which time the number of potential new members is whittled down to the players who can demonstrate the best lacrosse skill. Tryouts will be full gear and full speed and will likely consist of a variety of drills, with a full field scrimmage concluding each day.

For information about specific tryout dates, keep checking this page. New players interested in trying out are encouraged to come to talk to the team at Club Sports Night, which is held at the beginning of each semester in the Carpenter Sports Building. For any questions, new players should see the contact section of this website to get in touch with the president of the team. Cost

Because Delaware Lacrosse Club is student-run, we are also self funded. We endeavor to keep dues for players as low as possible to make the team accesible to as many people as we can. First year players can expect to pay about $400, while returning players will pay about $300 per year. This money is used to pay the cost of uniforms, referees, and fields for our games. For specific information about the cost of dues, contact us.

Playing for DLC

Delaware Lacrosse Club competes in the National College Lacrosse League, which means we play clubs from other Universities in the mid-Atlantic area, just like an NCAA lacrosse team. Our team provides a real college lacrosse experience while allowing students to balance other aspects of their college life that would be nearly impossible on an NCAA team.

Players should expect to play two tournaments during the fall semester and about ten games in the spring. The NCLL also hosts a playoff tournament at the conclusion of the spring season. Our goal as a team each year is to win the national championship.

Find Us

Home games and practices are held at a few locations around campus, detailed here.

New Turf and Frazer Field

Located at 26 N College Ave, Newark, DE 19716, Frazer Field and the New Turf are located behind the carpenter sports building, and are used for both practices and games. Metered parking is sometimes available in the lots off of North College Avenue, otherwise parking can always be found on the eastern end of East Main Street in the parking garage across from Deer Park Tavern.

Field House

The Field House is an indoor practice facility located on the southern end of campus between the football stadium and the ice arena. Located at 621 S College Ave Newark, DE 19716, parking is always available in the lot directly in front of the building.
